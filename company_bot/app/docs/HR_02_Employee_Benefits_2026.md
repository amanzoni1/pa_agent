## 2.0 MANDATORY HEALTH INSURANCE: BLUESHIELD GOLD PLATINUM PLAN

### 2.1 CORE COVERAGE SPECIFICATIONS
All full-time employees (FTEs ≥ 30 hours/week) are **automatically enrolled** in the *BlueShield Gold Platinum Plan* on their first day of employment. This plan represents Acme Corp’s commitment to eliminating financial barriers to critical healthcare access.

| Benefit Tier          | Coverage Details                                                                 | Employee Cost | Network Requirement       |
|-----------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------|---------------------------|
| **Primary Medical**   | 100% covered (pre-deductible) for in-network essential services                 | $0            | BlueShield Gold PPO Network |
| **Specialist Visits** | 100% covered (pre-deductible)                                                  | $0            | In-Network Only           |
| **Emergency Care**    | 100% covered globally (post-stabilization care must transition to in-network) | $0            | N/A (Global Coverage)     |
| **Prescription Drugs**| Tier 1-2: 100% covered; Tier 3/Specialty: 20% co-insurance                     | $0/$copay     | In-Network Pharmacy       |

> **CRITICAL CLARIFICATIONS**
> - **"100% covered"** explicitly means **$0 employee out-of-pocket costs** for deductibles, co-pays, and coinsurance on *in-network essential services* (defined per ACA §1302).
> - Out-of-network care incurs standard PPO cost-sharing (20% coinsurance after $1,500 deductible). **Pre-authorization is mandatory for non-emergent out-of-network services.**
> - Coverage extends to spouse/domestic partner and dependent children ≤ 26 years.

### 2.2 ENROLLMENT & ADMINISTRATION PROTOCOLS
1. **New Hires**: Enrollment is automatic. ID cards issued via BlueShield portal within 5 business days.
2. **Dependent Additions**: Submit *Form HR-BEN-202 (Dependent Verification)* with legal documentation (e.g., marriage certificate, birth certificate) within 30 days of qualifying life event.
3. **Claims Inquiry**: Contact BlueShield directly at 1-800-ACME-BSG (226-3274) or via [BlueShield Member Portal](https://members.blueshieldgold.acme.corp). **Acme HR does not process medical claims.**

## 3.0 DENTAL INSURANCE: TOOTHFAIRY PRO ELITE PLAN

### 3.1 COVERAGE SCOPE & LIMITATIONS
Enrollment in *ToothFairy Pro Elite* is **optional but highly encouraged**. Acme Corp subsidizes 80% of the premium ($45.00/month employee contribution). Coverage begins on the 1st of the month following enrollment.

| Service Type               | Annual Coverage Details                                  | Employee Cost |
|----------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|---------------|
| **Preventive Care**        | 2 routine cleanings (prophylaxis), exams, X-rays         | $0            |
| **Basic Procedures**       | Fillings, extractions: 80% coverage (after $50 deductible)| 20%           |
| **Major Procedures**       | Crowns, bridges, implants: 50% coverage                  | 50%           |
| **Orthodontics**           | 50% coverage (lifetime max: $2,000)                      | 50%           |

> **KEY POLICY DIRECTIVES**
> - **"2 cleanings"** are defined as two (2) prophylaxis sessions per calendar year (January 1–December 31). Unused cleanings **do not roll over**.
> - Pre-treatment estimates **must** be submitted for procedures >$300 via *ToothFairy Provider Portal*. Failure to obtain approval voids coverage.
> - Maximum annual benefit: $2,500 per enrollee.

### 3.2 CLAIMS & PROVIDER MANAGEMENT
- **In-Network Providers**: Locate via [ToothFairy Pro Provider Search](https://providers.toothfairypro.acme.corp).
- **Claim Submission**: Providers bill ToothFairy directly. For out-of-pocket expenses, submit *Form DENT-101* with itemized receipt to HR within 90 days.
- **Disputes**: Escalate via HR Benefits Team with documentation. Resolution timeframe: 15 business days.

## 4.0 WELLNESS & MENTAL HEALTH BENEFITS

### 4.1 GLOBALGYM MEMBERSHIP REIMBURSEMENT PROGRAM
Acme Corp **fully subsidizes** membership at *GlobalGym*, the world’s largest fitness network (12,000+ locations). This is **not** a stipend—it is a direct payment to GlobalGym.

#### ELIGIBILITY & ACTIVATION STEPS:
1. **Enroll**: Complete *Form WELL-303 (Gym Enrollment)* via HR ServiceNow portal.
2. **Select Location**: Choose any GlobalGym facility (standard or premium tier).
3. **Acme Corp Payment**: We pay GlobalGym directly monthly. **No reimbursement requests accepted.**
4. **ID Card**: Receive digital GlobalGym member ID via email within 72 hours of enrollment.

> **NON-NEGOTIABLE REQUIREMENTS**
> - Membership must be under the employee’s legal name.
> - Cancellation requires 30-day written notice to HR via *Form WELL-304*.
> - **Fraud Alert**: Misuse (e.g., family member using your membership as primary) triggers immediate termination + recovery of costs.

### 4.2 MENTAL HEALTH DAY POLICY: "ZEN-DAY" INITIATIVE
Recognizing the operational imperative of cognitive resilience, Acme Corp mandates **one (1) fully paid Mental Health Day per calendar month** with **zero justification required**.

#### POLICY EXECUTION PROTOCOL:
- **Eligibility**: All FTEs after 90-day probation.
- **Frequency**: 1 day/month (12 days/year). Unused days **expire monthly** (no accrual).
- **Request Process**:
  1. Submit time-off request in *Workday* > **Time Tracking** > **Absence Types**.
  2. **Select Code: `ZEN-DAY`** (critical for system tracking).
  3. **No manager approval needed**. System auto-approves.
  4. Submit **at least 24 hours in advance** (emergency exceptions require same-day HR email notification).
- **Confidentiality**: `ZEN-DAY` usage appears only as "Paid Time Off" on manager dashboards. **HR maintains encrypted logs; disclosure violates Acme Corp Privacy Policy §5.2.**

> **STRICT PROHIBITIONS**
> - `ZEN-DAY` **cannot** be used for vacation, sick leave (illness), or bereavement.
> - Managers **must not** inquire about the reason for use. Violations trigger mandatory ethics retraining.
> - Abuse (e.g., pattern of Monday/Friday usage without medical certification) subject to review by Chief Medical Officer.
